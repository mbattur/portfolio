A+ Nail School is one of Chicago's best nail school that provides high education for students who wants to improve their life and change their future for better.

Collaborating with the client I was able to recreate their exciting website and deliver user-friendly, colorful website for their users. In order to provide information also to promote the school, I rearranged and added contents to give more details about the school's goals and mission.

I looked at the issues that my client and their users were facing and identified the issues as:

  1. Misplaced and unnecessary contents
  2. The navigations are confusing
  3. Email form doesn’t work properly
While the whole website got a refresh, the changes are most clear in the navigation bar and the contents of every page. After lots of reading and doing some research I puzzled together all the related information and group them so users can easily find all the information they need in one place.
